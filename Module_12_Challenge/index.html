Overview of the Belly Button "Beef" Project

Are you a vegetarian or vegan who's been craving beef-like hamburgers? Or maybe you've been trying to eat less red meat but you can't give up those delicious hamburgers. A group of scientists at Improbable Beef and a group of trailblazing volunteers is on the job! Bacteria is being used to create meatless "beef." You read that correctly - bacteria! Improbable Beef is searching for the best bacteria to make delicious meatless meat. They've narrowed down the list of bacteria to those found in the human belly button. Our trailblazing volunteers offered up their bacteria samples and we're anxiously awaiting the results! Check out the information below to find out what kind of bacteria our volunteers have and stay tuned to find what bacteria wins the prize!
